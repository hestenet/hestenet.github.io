Fog & Fireflies

Pre-orders opening soon!

Ogma is a stern child of fourteen seasons whose village is adrift in a sea of fog. Strange phantoms roam outside the walls, their touch deadly... but only to adults. And so it is left to children to guard the walls. But children grow up—and when the oldest boy on the wall tries to pass his leadership to Ogma, she refuses, afraid of what it means.

Before she can process her feelings about the responsibility she’s denied, an attack comes that the children are not prepared for. This attack comes not from fog-phantoms, but people—people who've come to take them away.  The children fight back with what meager strength they have, but in the confusion Ogma finds herself cut off, outside the walls as the fog carries the village away. She is lost.

Being the pragmatic sort, Ogma knows she can't rely on anyone else to find her, so she resolves to find herself.
